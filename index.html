Technology Overview

The Third Eye device employs cutting-edge technology to enhance the mobility of visually impaired individuals. It utilizes ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in real-time, providing users with immediate feedback through audible alerts.

This technology not only helps in avoiding obstacles but also allows users to navigate their environment with greater confidence. The device is lightweight, portable, and designed to integrate seamlessly with existing mobility aids.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How does the Third Eye device work?

The Third Eye uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in the user's path and provides audible alerts to help navigate safely.

2. Is it easy to use?

Yes! The device is designed to be user-friendly, allowing individuals to integrate it easily with their existing mobility aids.

3. What is the cost of the device?

The estimated cost of the Third Eye device is under $25, making it an affordable solution for many users.
